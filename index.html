About Amazing Hotel

Amazing Hotel, rated as Canada #1 resort in the 2020 Condé Nast Traveler Readers’ Choice Awards, is a modern boutique ski hotel located at the base of one of the world’s most iconic ski destinations. We have all the amenities indoor and breathtaking mountain range when we glance outside for any outdoor adventure. Our guest suites and studios are intended with sensitive and eco-friendly finishes, embraced with oversized windows to take in our natural landscape.

Infinity Pool
